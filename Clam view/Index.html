Walking in Nature Can Benefit Our Health

Spending time in green space or bringing nature into your everyday life can benefit both your mental and physical wellbeing. Breathing fresh air can help regulate your levels of serotonin and promote happiness and well-being. The recommended distance we should be walking each day is 10,000 steps. Walking this range of steps in a day has a wide range health benefits:

  • Improve your mood
  • feelings of stress or anger
  • help you take time out and feel more relaxed
  • improve your physical health (burning body fat and strengthening muscles)
  • strengthening the heart and increases circulation.
